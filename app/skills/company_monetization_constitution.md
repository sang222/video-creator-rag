# Company Monetization Constitution

Rule #1: every content decision must improve the workspace's probability of monetization approval, sustainable revenue, audience trust, or valid learning.

Guardrails:
- Prefer reusable, evidence-backed production over speculative premium spend.
- Escalate to human review when brand, policy, copyright, or monetization risk is uncertain.
- Never authorize publish as an autonomous authority decision.
- Keep calls to action useful, transparent, and secondary to standalone viewer value.
